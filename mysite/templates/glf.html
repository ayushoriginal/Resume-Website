A search engine for services for the intellectually disabled people in US.

This project was done in collaboration with students from two other international institutions - Penn State University, US and Serajevo Institute of Technology, Bosnia. A major challenge in the project was to work from distributed locations across the globe. Co-ordination was extremely difficult owing to the difference in time zone.

The project involved developing a web app that can match an potential service seeker (an intellectually disabled user) to a service-provider who can satisfy user's need.
